3D Tile Visualization

At the heart of our platform is cutting-edge 3D tile visualization technology designed specifically for homeowners, interior designers, and architects. Whether you're renovating your kitchen, bathroom, living room, or any space, our service allows you to upload room photos or use pre-built templates to virtually place and view hundreds of tile designs in stunning 3D. No more guesswork—see exactly how your chosen tiles will look under different lighting, angles, and layouts before making a purchase.

Our extensive library features over 1,000+ tile designs, including ceramic, porcelain, marble, mosaic, and custom patterns in various colors, sizes, and textures. From modern minimalist to classic ornate styles, we cater to every aesthetic preference. Our intuitive tools let you rotate views, zoom in for details, and even simulate real-world wear and tear to ensure your selection is perfect for long-term use.

How It Works

Getting started is simple: Create a free account, upload your room dimensions or photo, select from our design catalog, and watch as our AI-powered engine generates photorealistic 3D renders. Fine-tune layouts, mix patterns, and experiment with combinations until you find the perfect match. Once satisfied, download high-resolution images, 360° views, or even AR previews for on-site visualization. Our premium subscriptions unlock unlimited projects, advanced editing tools, and priority support for professional users.

We partner with leading tile manufacturers to ensure our designs reflect real products available for purchase, making the transition from virtual to actual installation seamless. Whether you're a DIY enthusiast or a professional, our service saves time, reduces waste, and boosts confidence in your flooring decisions.
